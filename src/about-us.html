    The Origin

    Egyptian Saudi Company

    The Egyptian Saudi Company for Natural Water and Food Industries (ESC) is an investment group that was established in 2014 with an aim of heralding a new era for beverages in Egypt and the Middle East. The company has continuously sought to bring its signature brands to the market through using cutting-edge technologies that emphasize the importance of sustainability. Everything about ESC prioritizes functionality and healthy living. In doing so, the company strives to push the envelope on a global scale.

    The uniqueness behind the company’s purified approach to meeting people’s needs has likewise allowed it to introduce other celebrated brands to the market — with established names such as Sunny Supermarket, ESC for Juices, ESC for River Transport , and FLO providing considerable value to society in recent times.
